About

Dr. Jabar Aziz Baig completed his Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering at Memorial University of Newfoundland, NL, Canada in 2024. From 2012 to 2018, he was a Lecturer at Mirpur University of Science and Technology (MUST), Mirpur, Azad Jammu and Kashmir, Pakistan. In July 2010, he joined the Electricity department in Azad State of Jammu and Kashmir, Pakistan and served as assistant engineer electrical. His additional roles and experience include executive director of external affairs at the Graduate Student Union at Memorial University of Newfoundland, NL, Canada and member of the Senate at Memorial University, Canada.
